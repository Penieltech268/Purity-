SEXUAL PURITY

WHAT IS PURITY?

Purity is the state of being pure. It is living uncontaminated.

WHAT IS SEXUAL PURITY?

Sexual purity is the abstinence from all sexual perversion, impure thoughts, and gratification from the desires of intimacy with the opposite sex.
